Meter Reader Recognition

ShiJie Sun

Description

In some cases, the meter cannot use internal electronic devices. Therefore, using the external visual device is the necessary way of meter reader automation. In this project, we leverage a mini-camera and the Embedded Device (OpenMV) to automatically read the meter. I am responsible for algorithm design. The final product can achieve 4 fps within the 1% error.
